QuantCoderFS

Modular AI workflows for alpha research in finance.
Local-first. No login. No cloud.

Chat with Fundamentals

Transform financial queries into structured research notes using EODHD data and large language models. Includes ratio analysis, OHLCV history and news filtering.

Search Articles

LLM-enhanced agents expand queries across arXiv and CrossRef, returning matched papers with titles, abstracts, and source metadata.

Summary Generation

Extract and restructure academic papers into concise, sectioned Markdown summaries – perfect for research documentation or Notion export.

Code Generation

Convert research summaries into QuantConnect-ready Python strategies with syntax validation, logic modelling, and agent-based refinement.
